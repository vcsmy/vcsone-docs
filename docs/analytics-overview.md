## Executive Summary

VCS One Analytics combines ETL, forecasting, anomaly detection, and real-time triggers in one composable platform. Designed for organizations seeking actionable business intelligence beyond passive dashboards.

### Target Audience
- Data-driven organizations
- Operations teams automating decisions
- Business intelligence users
- Non-technical decision makers
